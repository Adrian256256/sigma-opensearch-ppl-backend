# Testing Sigma Rules on Generated Security Logs

This folder contains testing queries for the `security-logs` index generated by `generate_logs.py`.
The dataset contains 500 synthetic Windows security events including process creation, DNS queries, and network connections.

## Generated Logs Dataset

The synthetic logs include Sigma original field names (Image, CommandLine, QueryName, etc.), ensuring direct compatibility with queries generated by `sigma-ppl`.

**Total logs indexed:** 500  
**Malicious logs included:** ~150 (30%)  
**Benign logs included:** ~350 (70%)

## Tested and Validated Sigma Rules

The following official Sigma rules from `ecs_fields_info/sigma-master` return results when queried against this dataset:

### 1. Mimikatz Execution - Command Line Detection

**Rule File**: `ecs_fields_info/sigma-master/rules/windows/process_creation/proc_creation_win_hktl_mimikatz_command_line.yml`

**Convert Sigma Rule to PPL**:
```bash
./cli/sigma-ppl ecs_fields_info/sigma-master/rules/windows/process_creation/proc_creation_win_hktl_mimikatz_command_line.yml
```

**Generated PPL Query**:
```ppl
source=windows-process_creation-* | where LIKE(CommandLine, "%DumpCreds%") OR LIKE(CommandLine, "%mimikatz%") OR LIKE(CommandLine, "%::aadcookie%") OR LIKE(CommandLine, "%::detours%") OR LIKE(CommandLine, "%::memssp%") OR LIKE(CommandLine, "%::mflt%") OR LIKE(CommandLine, "%::ncroutemon%") OR LIKE(CommandLine, "%::ngcsign%") OR LIKE(CommandLine, "%::printnightmare%") OR LIKE(CommandLine, "%::skeleton%") OR LIKE(CommandLine, "%::preshutdown%") OR LIKE(CommandLine, "%::mstsc%") OR LIKE(CommandLine, "%::multirdp%") OR LIKE(CommandLine, "%rpc::%") OR LIKE(CommandLine, "%token::%") OR LIKE(CommandLine, "%crypto::%") OR LIKE(CommandLine, "%dpapi::%") OR LIKE(CommandLine, "%sekurlsa::%") OR LIKE(CommandLine, "%kerberos::%") OR LIKE(CommandLine, "%lsadump::%") OR LIKE(CommandLine, "%privilege::%") OR LIKE(CommandLine, "%process::%") OR LIKE(CommandLine, "%vault::%")
```

### 2. Suspicious Scheduled Task Patterns

**Rule File**: `ecs_fields_info/sigma-master/rules/windows/process_creation/proc_creation_win_schtasks_susp_pattern.yml`

**Convert Sigma Rule to PPL**:
```bash
./cli/sigma-ppl ecs_fields_info/sigma-master/rules/windows/process_creation/proc_creation_win_schtasks_susp_pattern.yml
```

**Generated PPL Query**:
```ppl
source=windows-process_creation-* | where LIKE(Image, "%schtasks.exe") AND LIKE(CommandLine, "%/Create %") AND ((LIKE(CommandLine, "%/sc minute %") OR LIKE(CommandLine, "%/ru system %")) AND (LIKE(CommandLine, "%cmd /c%") OR LIKE(CommandLine, "%cmd /k%") OR LIKE(CommandLine, "%cmd /r%") OR LIKE(CommandLine, "%cmd.exe /c %") OR LIKE(CommandLine, "%cmd.exe /k %") OR LIKE(CommandLine, "%cmd.exe /r %")) OR LIKE(CommandLine, "% -decode %") OR LIKE(CommandLine, "% -enc %") OR LIKE(CommandLine, "% -w hidden %") OR LIKE(CommandLine, "% bypass %") OR LIKE(CommandLine, "% IEX%") OR LIKE(CommandLine, "%.DownloadData%") OR LIKE(CommandLine, "%.DownloadFile%") OR LIKE(CommandLine, "%.DownloadString%") OR LIKE(CommandLine, "%/c start /min %") OR LIKE(CommandLine, "%FromBase64String%") OR LIKE(CommandLine, "%mshta http%") OR LIKE(CommandLine, "%mshta.exe http%") OR (LIKE(CommandLine, "%:ProgramData%") OR LIKE(CommandLine, "%:Temp%") OR LIKE(CommandLine, "%:Tmp%") OR LIKE(CommandLine, "%:UsersPublic%") OR LIKE(CommandLine, "%:WindowsTemp%") OR LIKE(CommandLine, "%AppData%") OR LIKE(CommandLine, "%\%AppData\%%") OR LIKE(CommandLine, "%\%Temp\%%") OR LIKE(CommandLine, "%\%tmp\%%")) AND (LIKE(CommandLine, "%cscript%") OR LIKE(CommandLine, "%curl%") OR LIKE(CommandLine, "%wscript%")))
```

### 3. PsExec Service Execution

**Rule File**: `ecs_fields_info/sigma-master/rules/windows/process_creation/proc_creation_win_renamed_sysinternals_psexec_service.yml`

**Convert Sigma Rule to PPL**:
```bash
./cli/sigma-ppl ecs_fields_info/sigma-master/rules/windows/process_creation/proc_creation_win_renamed_sysinternals_psexec_service.yml
```

**Generated PPL Query**:
```ppl
source=windows-process_creation-* | where OriginalFileName="psexesvc.exe" AND NOT Image="C:WindowsPSEXESVC.exe"
```

### 4. Suspicious Calculator Usage

**Rule File**: `ecs_fields_info/sigma-master/rules/windows/process_creation/proc_creation_win_calc_uncommon_exec.yml`

**Convert Sigma Rule to PPL**:
```bash
./cli/sigma-ppl ecs_fields_info/sigma-master/rules/windows/process_creation/proc_creation_win_calc_uncommon_exec.yml
```

**Generated PPL Query**:
```ppl
source=windows-process_creation-* | where LIKE(CommandLine, "%calc.exe %") OR LIKE(Image, "%calc.exe") AND NOT (LIKE(Image, "%:WindowsSystem32%") OR LIKE(Image, "%:WindowsSysWOW64%") OR LIKE(Image, "%:WindowsWinSxS%"))
```
